About Us

Filmmaking is the ultimate team sport

Micheal Keaton.

AlivCinemastudio, envisioned by BK. Harish Babu, was born from the dream of a team of filmmakers, editors, and technicians in Chennai. It all began with two creative minds, deeply passionate about storytelling, who dreamed of making a lasting impact through visual narratives.

Now, with over 100 films created, we take pride in being *Alive*—alive with the vibrant creative space we've built, alive with the filmmaking process, and deeply passionate about collaborating with others who share our vision for impactful storytelling.

At AlivCinemastudio, we believe that a story can be told in countless ways, but it’s only when we embrace creative freedom that the true magic unfolds.
