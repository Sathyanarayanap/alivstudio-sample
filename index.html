About Us

Micheal Keaton

Filmmaking is the ultimate team sport

AlivCinemastudio, envisioned by BK. Harish Babu, was born from the dream of a team of filmmakers, editors, and technicians in Chennai.
It all began with two creative minds, deeply passionate about storytelling, who dreamed of making a lasting impact through visual narratives.

Now, with over 100 films created, we take pride in being *Alive*—alive with the vibrant creative space we've built, alive with the filmmaking process.
We are deeply passionate about collaborating with others who share our vision for impactful storytelling.

At AlivCinemastudio, we believe that a story can be told in countless ways, but it’s only when we embrace creative freedom that the true magic unfolds.

Sound Effects-SFX

At AlivCinemaStudio, we specialize in delivering a diverse collection of professional sound effects tailored to elevate your project.
Whether you need custom-designed audio to match your unique vision or high-quality pre-made libraries, we've got you covered.
Our goal is to bring your creative ideas to life through immersive, high-impact sound that resonates with your audience.

Social media

Let us help you tell your story with creativity and impact.
